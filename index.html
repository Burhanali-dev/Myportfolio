I thrived in these fast paced environments by problem solving in high pressure situations and communicating empathetically with a diverse patient population. I was the first Somali EMT for the City of Boston, and my dedication was recognized by Mayor Marty Walsh. As a software engineer, my unique life and career experiences will make me a valuable member of your company.
